Who We Are

Welcome to Sainik Public School

Sainik Public School is a premier educational institution rooted in the noble tradition of the Sainik ethos — discipline, integrity, and service to the nation. We offer holistic education from Nursery to Class XII, nurturing young minds with academic rigor, moral values, and all-round development.

Our school fosters a close partnership between students, teachers, and parents — creating a learning environment where every child can discover their unique strengths and grow into confident, responsible citizens.

A Word From Our Principal

"

Honing a child is perhaps the biggest responsibility for all. It requires an environment that consistently nurtures the young mind, body and spirit — metamorphosing them into strong human beings capable of holding their own in the world outside.

At Sainik Public School, we believe that parents and teachers play the most important roles in the learning years of a child. The school encourages a close partnership between the trinity of learning — the child, the teacher, and the parent.
